Meet Our Directors

Ryan Foret, Director of Bands

foretr@mcnairy.org

Ryan Foret is in his 25th year as Director of Bands at McNairy Central High School. Before Coming to Selmer, Mr. Foret received his BME from Louisiana State University, where he studied with Frank Wickes. He was a member of the LSU Tiger Band for five years. In addition to the Tiger band, Mr. Foret performed with the Wind Ensemble, Wind Ensemble II, Symphonic Band, Louisiana Collegiate Honor Band, and two brass quintets. He was also a member of the Expressions Drum and Bugle Corps for two years and the World Champion Cavaliers Drum and Bugle Corps for two years. He is currently the principal trumpet for the Corinth Symphony Orchestra. During his time at MCHS, Mr. Foret has been nominated to National Honor Society teacher award (2 years), Who’s Who Among American High School Teachers (7 years), and was the 2012 McNairy Central and McNairy County Teacher of the Year.a founding board member of AIM, and MCHS fine arts department chair. He also has 164 grand championship awards and has been a Tennessee State Division II finalist for the last 18 years. He is an active member of WTSBOA, TMEA, MENC and TBMA. Mr. Foret also teaches in the mornings at Bethel Springs School and teaches General Music and Theater during the day.

Rebbecca Kerr, Assistant Director and Instructor to WoodWind Ensemble

Rebecca Kerr is in her 23rdd year as assistant director of bands at McNairy Central and head director at Selmer Middle School. Before coming to Selmer, Ms. Kerr was band director and elementary music teacher in Benton County for two years and taught elementary music for three years at Milan Elementary. Ms. Kerr received her BMME from The University of Tennessee at Martin where she studied clarinet with Gilbert Carp. She also has her Masters degree in educational studies from Nazarene Trevecca

Isaac Spitler, Assistant Director and Instructor of High Brass

Graduated The University of Tennessee at Martin in Spring 2019 majoring in Music Education. He is currently the Music teacher at Selmer Elementary, and the Instructor of High Brass of The McNairy Central Bobcat Band since 2019.

Trice Mayhall, Assistant Director and Instructor of Percussion

Kassie Parrish, Color Guard Instructor

Kassie Parrish is in her 12th year of teaching. She has been with the Bobcat Band all three of these years. She is a graduate of McNairy Central High School and a recent graduate of Northeast Mississippi Community College, where she received her Associate’s Degree in Radiologic Technology. She was a member of the Bobcat Band for two years, and a part of the Northeast Independent Winterguard who brought home the MIA State Championship in 2012. She was recently a part of Magnolia Independent, who won the SCGC Championship this year. Magnolia Independent was also the first Independent Winter Guard to compete at WGI Championships in Dayton, Ohio.
