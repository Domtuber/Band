About the Brooke Pope Scholarship

The McNairy Central Bobcat Band and Boosters are proud to have founded the Brooke Lauren Pope Memorial Scholarship. Brooke was a musician in the Selmer Middle and McNairy Central High School Bands for 7 years. She was a proud member of the MCHS Drumline and also served as a mentor to many younger musicians. Brookeâ€™s talent, intellect, maturity, and radiant smile made her a beloved member of the band. She will never be forgotten. Each spring, the scholarship will be awarded to a senior band member who plans on attending any college or university in the fall semester. Any donations to the scholarship should be sent to McNairy Central High School and will be restricted with express purpose of use for the Brooke Lauren Pope Memorial Scholarship Fund.
